Welcome to UniHub

A simple and dedicated platform developed for our college students to connect, learn, and grow together. Seniors guide juniors, students share resources, and everyone benefits.

Join Our Community

Purpose of This Website

This platform was built as a student mini project to solve a real problem: the gap between seniors and juniors. Often, juniors need help with academics, projects, or placement preparation but hesitate to ask. Seniors have valuable experience and materials but lack a centralized place to share them. This platform bridges that gap by offering a safe, academic-focused community environment.

Platform Features

Discussion Forum

Ask academic doubts, discuss programming concepts, and get answers directly from your experienced seniors.

Study Resources

Access and upload study materials, previous year examination papers, and detailed placement preparation guides.

Project Teams

Looking for teammates for your next hackathon or embedded systems mini project? Find them here.

Internships & Events

Stay updated with the latest on-campus and off-campus internships, college fests, and technical workshops.
